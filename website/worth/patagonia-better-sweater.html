Frequently asked questions

Are older Patagonia fleeces worth more?

Some discontinued colorways and vintage Synchilla pieces command a premium, but most Better Sweaters resell on condition and color rather than age.

Does Patagonia's warranty affect resale?

Yes — Patagonia's Ironclad Guarantee and repair service give buyers confidence, which supports higher secondhand prices than comparable fleeces.
