Do worn Birkenstocks sell?

Yes, at a discount. A molded footbed reduces value but many buyers seek broken-in pairs; cork and strap condition matter most.

Which Birkenstock styles resell best?

Arizona and Boston in suede or shearling-lined versions, plus limited colors, tend to hold value best.
