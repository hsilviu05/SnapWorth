How much is a Vintage Band T-Shirt worth to resell?

Vintage band tees are one of the most variable — and lucrative — resale categories. Era, band, and tour dates can swing value from tens to hundreds.

Typical resale value: $30–$200

Ranges reflect typical US secondhand prices and are a guide, not an appraisal. Actual value varies by demand, timing, and platform fees.

What affects the resale value of a Vintage Band T-Shirt

  • Band and tour rarity
  • Era (single-stitch, tag brand like Brockum/Giant)
  • Fades, holes, and 'thrashed' appeal
  • Size (larger sells higher)
  • Original vs. reprint

Frequently asked questions

How do I know if a band tee is truly vintage?

Single-stitch hems, tag brands like Brockum, Giant, or Anvil, and a soft, worn feel indicate true vintage. Reprints use modern double-stitch and tags.

Which band tees sell for the most?

Rare metal, rap, and grunge tour tees from the '80s–'90s command the highest prices, especially in larger sizes.
