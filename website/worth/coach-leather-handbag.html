How much is a Coach Leather Handbag worth to resell?

Coach leather handbags are a reliable resale category. Vintage 'Made in USA' bags and full-leather styles outperform coated-canvas pieces.

Frequently asked questions

Are vintage Coach bags valuable?

Many vintage all-leather Coach bags (e.g. the Willis or Bonnie Cashin era) resell well above modern coated-canvas styles.

How do I authenticate a Coach bag?

Check the interior creed patch and serial number, stitching quality, and hardware. Photos of these details raise buyer trust and price.
