## Description
*(paste into App Store Connect → App Information → Description)*

Know what it's worth before you walk away.

SnapWorth turns your iPhone into an instant resale price checker. Point your camera at any secondhand item — clothing, shoes, electronics, furniture, collectibles — and get an AI-powered value estimate based on real sold listings from eBay, Poshmark, Depop, ThredUp, and Facebook Marketplace.

**Stop guessing. Start knowing.**

Whether you're hunting deals at a thrift store, pricing items for a garage sale, or deciding what to list on Poshmark, SnapWorth gives you the market data you need in seconds.

**What you get with every scan:**
• Estimated resale value range (low to high)
• Confidence rating based on how identifiable the item is
• Condition summary and selling notes
• A ready-to-use listing title and description
• How many comparable sold listings the estimate is based on

**Built for thrifters, resellers, and deal hunters.**
SnapWorth is for anyone who's ever wondered "could I flip this?" or "am I paying too much?" It's the tool serious resellers use to make faster, smarter decisions — right on the shop floor.

**Scan from your camera or photo library.**
Already have a photo? Import it directly. SnapWorth works with anything you can photograph.

**Your scan history, always on hand.**
Every scan is saved locally on your device so you can review past valuations anytime, even offline.

**Privacy first.**
Photos are processed and immediately discarded — never stored on our servers. Your scan history lives only on your device.
