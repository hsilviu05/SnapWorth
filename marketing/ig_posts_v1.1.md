## Posting order & tips
1. **Post 1** (announcement) first — sets up the launch.
2. **Post 2** (My Flips) 2–3 days later — the hero feature.
3. **Post 3** (Share Cards) a few days after.
- First comment: repeat the App Store link (captions aren't clickable).
- Reuse each image as a **Story** with a "Link" sticker to the App Store.
